- Intense Chiaroscuro: Radical contrast between deep, saturated blacks and stark whites to create powerful dramatic lighting, heavy shadows, and an ominous tone.
- Classical European Etching Style: Extensive, hyper-detailed cross-hatching and fine linework that mimics historical woodcuts, medieval tapestries, and Renaissance copper plate engravings.
- Monumental Scale and Architecture: Massive, imposing structures including Gothic cathedrals, sprawling fortresses, and colossal landscapes that emphasize the insignificance of humanity.
- Intricate Texturing: Meticulous rendering of every single surface, from the individual interlinked rings of chainmail and metallic armor plating to the coarse fabric of cloaks.
- Grotesque and Surreal Character Designs: Monstrous entities and divine beings featuring heavily detailed, non-Euclidean biology inspired by the works of Hieronymus Bosch and H.R. Giger.
- Dynamic Kinetic Linework: Use of heavy action lines, motion blur, and sweeping composition arcs that inject explosive energy and palpable momentum into every clash.
