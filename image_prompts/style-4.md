- Masterful Brushwork: Drawn primarily using traditional Japanese ink brushes (Menso fude) instead of a standard G-pen, creating elegant, organic variations in line thickness.
- Hyper-Realistic Anatomy: Exceptional command over human proportions, capturing intricate details of muscles, bone structures, knuckles, and accurate facial expressions.
- Micro-Hatching and Texturing: Extremely fine, dense cross-hatching utilized on skin, clothing, and weaponry to give characters a weighted, three-dimensional presence.
- Sumi-e Ink Wash Effects: Heavy use of expressive ink spatters, diluted gray washes, and raw strokes to depict chaotic motion, blood splatters, and environmental weather.
- Cinematic Framing and Scale: Dramatic pacing featuring sweeping, photorealistic historical landscapes, intensely detailed close-ups, and panel compositions that mimic live-action tracking shots.
- Visceral Combat Dynamism: Action panels that rely on motion blur, sweeping brush dragging, and negative space to communicate the terrifying speed and lethal weight of a sword strike.
